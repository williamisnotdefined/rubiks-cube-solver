# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Contributors and maintainers agree to keep project spaces respectful, focused, and harassment-free.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Respect different experience levels and viewpoints.
- Focus criticism on code, behavior, and project outcomes.
- Disclose conflicts of interest when they affect technical decisions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of technical discussion.
- Sharing secrets, private captures, or exploit details in public channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or escalate to GitHub moderation when needed.
